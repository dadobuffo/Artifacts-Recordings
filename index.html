Artifacts Recordings is an online mixing and mastering studio for heavy music.

No studio bookings, no hourly rates, no limits - just a focused workflow built around your project and needs.

Every record gets the same attention: detailed work, direct communication, and an honest opinion when you need one.

Heavy, Tight, and Punchy mix.

ABOUT

I'm Dario Menik and I'm an audio engineer, producer, and sound designer.

Artifacts started small: helping local bands in Florence sound better than their budgets should have allowed. It grew from there, but the approach stayed the same — direct communication, honest feedback, and no wasted time.

I work from a treated home studio, which keeps things lean and focused. No studio clock ticking in the background — just the music.

Services
01

Production

From scratch or from your demos — arranging, programming drums, writing guitar layers, synths and atmospheres. Building the sonic architecture of your track before a single note is tracked.

  • Song arrangement & pre-production
  • Drum programming & editing
  • Guitar & bass layers
  • Synths, atmospheres & sound design
02

Mixing

Taking your raw recordings and sculpting them into a cohesive, powerful mix. Every element in its place, every frequency fighting for the right space. Clarity without sacrifice.

  • Stem & multitrack mixing
  • Vocal tuning & editing
  • Drum replacement & augmentation
  • Up to 3 revisions included
03

Mastering

The final step before release. Optimizing the stereo mix for all playback systems and streaming platforms, ensuring loudness, punch, and consistency across every format.

  • Streaming-optimized loudness
  • Vinyl & CD mastering available
  • ISRC / metadata embedding
  • Stems mastering on request
