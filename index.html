ABOUT ME

I'm Dario Menik ~ audio engineer, producer, and sound designer with 10+ years of experience in the music industry.

My background covers recording and mixing across a wide range of genres - from metal and hardcore to pop and hip-hop - as well as sound design and composition for video games. I hold a BA in Music Production and I've been a musician for most of my life.

I founded Artifacts Recordings in 2020 to focus on what I'm most passionate about: working with bands and artists who have something to say, with a particular focus on heavy music.

Services
01

Production

Building a track from scratch or developing an existing demo into a finished record. Covers arrangement, composition, and all instrumental layers before tracking begins.

  • Arrangement & song structure
  • Drum programming & sequencing
  • Guitar, bass & synth layers
  • Pre-production & demo development
02

Mixing

Balancing and processing a multitrack session into a tight stereo mix. Includes all editing, correction, and processing needed before mastering.

  • Stem & multitrack mixing
  • Editing, vocal pitch & timing correction
  • Drum replacement & augmentation
  • Hybrid analog/digital workflow
03

Mastering

Final processing of the stereo mix for distribution. Covers loudness optimization, tonal balance, and dynamics — delivered ready for streaming platforms and physical formats.

  • Streaming loudness compliance
  • Vinyl & CD pre-mastering
  • ISRC & metadata embedding
  • All formats on request
