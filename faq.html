Any Questions?

What is Artifacts Recordings?

Artifacts Recordings is an online post-production studio that specializes in heaviest music. I offer mixing and mastering services, as well as production and editing work, see the "services" section for more details.

Do you work with all genres?

My background as an audio engineer covers a wide range of genres, and I've worked across many of them professionally. Artifacts Recordings exists because I wanted to focus on the kind of music I'm most passionate about - but that doesn't mean I turn down everything else. If your project sounds interesting, reach out and we'll figure it out!

How can I prepare my project before sending it?

I've prepared a PDF where you can find some requirements and instructions in order to get the best sounding recordings. For any additional information, get in touch with me via the contact page.

How long does mixing take?

Mixing times can vary depending on the project and current workload, but I usually deliver the final master within a week.

How many revisions can I request?

All clients are provided a reasonable amount of revisions, all revisions must be requested within 10 days of final product delivery. Any revisions requested after 10 days are subject to additional charges.

How do payments work?

A 50% deposit is required to begin work. The remaining balance is due before final files are delivered. All payments are non-refundable. Final media will not be delivered until payment is made in full (unless otherwise arranged). You can pay by bank transfer. If PayPal must be used, then you will be charged for the commission and transfer fee that PayPal takes on top of service charge.

Can you record our band?

Artifacts Recordings is primarily an online studio - I work remotely with artists worldwide. For local bands based in Italy, I also offer on-site recording and tracking sessions. Get in touch to discuss what your project needs.

Do you provide stems?

Stems and separate tracks are included in the price but must be requested in advance.

What file formats do you deliver?

All files are delivered as 24-bit WAV. Additional formats - 32-bit WAV, MP3, FLAC, OPUS, or CD-ready masters - are available on request at no extra charge.
