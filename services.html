Services

Core services

I

Production

Building a track from scratch or developing an existing demo into a finished record. Covers arrangement, composition, and all instrumental layers before tracking begins.

  • Arrangement & song structure
  • Drum programming & sequencing
  • Guitar, bass & synth layers
  • Pre-production & demo development
II

Mixing

Balancing and processing a multitrack session into a tight stereo mix. Includes all editing, correction, and processing needed before mastering.

  • Stem & multitrack mixing
  • Editing, vocal pitch & timing correction
  • Drum replacement & augmentation
  • Hybrid analog/digital workflow
III

Mastering

Final processing of the stereo mix for distribution. Covers loudness optimization, tonal balance, and dynamics - delivered ready for streaming platforms and physical formats.

  • Streaming loudness compliance
  • Vinyl & CD pre-mastering
  • ISRC & metadata embedding
  • All formats on request

Additional services

IV

Tracking & Recording

On-site recording sessions from my studio. Available on request for guitars, bass, and vocals, with or without subsequent mixing.

  • Electric & acoustic guitar
  • Bass (direct & miked)
  • Lead & backing vocals
  • DI tracks available for reamping
V

Guitar Tone & Reamping

Tone design and amp selection for electric guitar and bass. From high-gain rhythm tones to clean and dynamic sounds, built around the track.

  • Amp sim & cabinet design
  • DI reamping & tone shaping
  • Blending & layering multiple tones
  • Delivered as high-res WAV stems
VI

Drum & Vocal Editing

Editing and cleanup of audio material before or after mixing. Covers timing, tuning, and technical correction across all tracks.

  • Drum quantization & timing
  • Vocal comping & pitch correction
  • Sample replacement & augmentation
  • Noise & breath cleanup
VII

MIDI Programming

Programming realistic MIDI performances for drums, orchestras, or other virtual instruments. Useful for demos, mockups, or production beds.

  • Drum programming & humanization
  • Orchestral & cinematic mockups
  • MIDI-to-audio rendering
  • Session export in any format
VIII

Session Musician

Remote recording of guitar and bass parts for your project. You provide the session files, I deliver tracked stems ready for mixing.

  • Electric guitar (rhythm & lead)
  • Bass (direct & miked)
  • Multiple takes provided
  • Delivered as DIs or reamped stems
IX

Audio Restoration

Repair and cleanup of damaged or degraded audio material. Useful for live recordings, archival material, or poorly captured sources.

  • Noise & hum reduction
  • Clipping & distortion repair
  • Room & reverb removal
  • Dialogue & vocal restoration
