Services

Every project is different. The services below cover the full scope of what I offer — from fully hands-on production to targeted post-production work. Not sure what you need? Get in touch and we'll figure it out together.

Core services

I

Production

Building a song from the ground up — or rescuing a demo that's almost there. I handle arrangement, composition, drum programming, guitar and bass layers, synthesis, and everything that defines the sonic identity of a track before a single mic goes up. Heavy music is the specialty, but the approach adapts to the project.

  • Full song arrangement & composition
  • Drum programming (Superior Drummer, Slate, samples)
  • Guitar, bass & keyboard layers
  • Synths, pads & atmospheric textures
  • Demo-to-release pipeline
  • Pre-production consulting
II

Mixing

Taking your recorded stems and sculpting them into a cohesive, powerful mix. Every frequency in its right place. Every element serving the song. Tight low end, aggressive mids, clear highs — the sound you've been hearing in your head.

  • Stem & multitrack mixing
  • Vocal pitch correction & timing
  • Drum replacement & sample augmentation
  • Bus & parallel processing
  • Up to 3 revision rounds included
  • Deliverables: WAV + stems on request
III

Mastering

The last pass before the world hears it. Mastering is about translation — making sure your music sounds its best on every playback system, at the right loudness, with the right dynamics. No brick-wall squashing: punch and clarity, together.

  • Streaming-optimized loudness (Spotify, Apple, YouTube)
  • Vinyl & CD pre-mastering
  • ISRC & metadata embedding
  • Stems mastering available
  • Delivery in all required formats
  • Album / EP sequencing & spacing

Additional services

IV

Audio Editing

Cleanup and editing for spoken word, podcasts, interviews, and corporate audio. Background noise removal, breath and filler reduction, timing correction, and delivery-ready files — without losing the natural flow of the conversation.

  • Podcast & interview editing
  • Noise reduction & cleanup
  • Music beds & transitions
  • Voiceover sync & timing
  • Delivery to broadcast specs
V

Tracking & Recording

On request, I can record guitars, bass, and vocals from my home studio — acoustically treated, well-equipped, and without the pressure of a ticking studio clock. A practical option if you don't have access to a recording space.

  • Electric & acoustic guitar
  • Bass (direct & miked)
  • Vocals (lead & backing)
  • DI tracks available for reamping

Available on request — contact me to discuss logistics.

VI

Reamping

Already recorded your guitars DI but not happy with the tone? Send me the tracks and I'll run them through real amps and cabs in my studio, capturing an organic, full sound that plugins rarely replicate.

  • Real amp + cab signal chain
  • Multiple takes / tones available
  • Blending with original DI or plugins
  • Delivery as high-res WAV stems
VII

Arrangement

Got a song idea, a riff, or a rough demo but don't know where to take it? I can develop your material into a fully arranged track — adding structure, dynamics, tension and release, and all the layers that turn an idea into a song.

  • Full or partial arrangement
  • Works from demos, voice memos, MIDI
  • Orchestration & harmonic development
  • Genre-appropriate instrumentation
VIII

Sound Design

Custom sound design for video, trailers, short films, and interactive media. From atmospheric textures and drones to hits, impacts, and transitions — sounds built around your visual content rather than cut from a library.

  • Original SFX design
  • Trailer & promo sound design
  • Atmospheric textures & drones
  • UI & game audio
  • Sync to picture
IX

Consultation

Not sure where your project stands or what it needs? A focused session where I listen to your material, give honest feedback, and help you figure out the next steps — whether that's production, mixing, re-tracking, or something else entirely.

  • Mix & production critique
  • Workflow & gear advice
  • Release preparation review
  • Available online (video call)
